June 15, 2024 in Daegu

Awesome Music Festival 2024 in Daegu Finally! I got to go see Day6 perform. I went to the music festival on my own, so I was quite worried. But now all I want to do is go to music festivals and concerts on my own all the time. I literally had the time of my life and had so much fun. The best festival I have ever been to. All the stages were so good and their live voices were the best. I appreciated how they really made sure we can hear the band's sounds clearly for the audience. There was even a firework performance for the encore song, which felt like a dream. I was jumping up and down and singing at the top of my lungs. Day6 songs helped me get through difficult times throughout my first year in school and living alone. It was so comforting and unreal to hear them live. My favorite songs from the festival:

  • Somehow
  • Healer
  • Sweet Chaos
  • Congratulations
  • Best Part
P.S. I almost didn’t make it to my train back, but I met the sweetest people to ride a taxi with. I will forever remember and be grateful for them.
